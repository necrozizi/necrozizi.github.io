Italy faces a sudden surge in covid-19 cases

Italy faces a sudden surge in covid-19 cases surge： With disconcerting suddenness, Italy has found itself the European country worst affected by covid-19. By February 23rd the number of people infected with the coronavirus that first emerged in the Chinese city of Wuhan had leapt to 152, jump of around 100 within a day. Three of them all in their late 60s or older, have already died. Italy now has the third-most cases in the world, after China itself, where 77,000 have been infected, and South Korea, where the total has surged to more than 600.
